Okinawa Churaumi Aquarium

The aquarium introduces you to the mystery of Okinawa’s marine creatures,with the naturally lit Coral Sea tank, dynamic Kuroshio Sea tank, where you can observe the world’s biggest fish as well as whale sharks and manta rays.And as you go deeper into the aquarium, you will encounter the Deep Sea tank,allowing you to experience Okinawa’s entire underwater world.
